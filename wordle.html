How to Play
Try to guess a 5-letter word. After you guess a word, each letter you type will turn into a tile. If the letter's tile is green, it is in the word but not in the right spot. If a tile is yellow, it is in the word but in the wrong spot. If the letter's tile is gray, it is not in the word.
